DTSE Writing for the Web

An Expedition to write, create, and code for the web

  • Web Design

  • Writing

    I write about mobile web design, responsive design and more on my blog and have contributed pieces to a number of publications, magazines and books.

  • Speaking

    I'm extremely passionate about what I do and love talking about where the Web is going. I speak about design, development and more at conferences around the world.

  • Workshops

    I give workshops about responsive web design, atomic design, and more and companies and conferences all over the world.

  • Consulting

    I help brands, agencies, and other organizations tackle the ever-shifting multi-device Web landscape.

  • This Is Responsive

    This Is Responsive is a site that collects resources, news and emerging patterns to help people create great responsive web experiences.

  • Sharing

    I love sharing information, learning loads of new stuff every day, and participating in the collective conversation that is Twitter.

  • Pattern Lab

    Dave Olsen and I created Pattern Lab, a collection of tools to help people create atomic web design systems.

  • WTF Mobile Web

    Co-curated with Jen Simmons, WTF Mobile Web collects examples of what not to do when creating web experiences in this mobile age.

  • WTF QR Codes

    QR codes can be hilarious. Craig Villamor and I collect the most ridiculous implementations of QR codes out in the wild.

  • Music

    I'm primarily a bass player, though I also enjoy playing drums, guitar and keyboards (tabletops when nothing else is available). I play and record with my wonderful wife.

  • Art

    Art has been a passion my whole life and I will always pursue it in some way shape or form. I enjoy painting, drawing, sculpting and designing.
